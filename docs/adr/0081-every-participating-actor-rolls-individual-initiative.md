# Every participating actor rolls individual Initiative

Every actor in Tactical Participation that receives a standard activation makes one individual Lattice-100 Initiative Roll for that Tactical Pressure, including actors participating in dangerous repairs, pursuits, contested interactions, and other noncombat pressures. Hazards and objectives receive deterministic timing entries instead of Initiative Rolls. This supersedes ADR-0034's combatant-only wording while preserving individual rather than side-based Initiative and a stable ordered turn sequence.
