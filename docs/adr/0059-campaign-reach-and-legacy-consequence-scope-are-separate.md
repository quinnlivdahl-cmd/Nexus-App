# Campaign Reach and Legacy Consequence scope are separate

A campaign may be a system-wide adventure that travels among distant bodies, spans several Regions, and connects operations across the setting. This Campaign Reach does not require setting-wide Legacy Consequences: durable world changes remain scoped to the factions, institutions, territories, relationships, public knowledge, resources, or conditions actually changed through committed play. Setting-wide changes remain rare because consequence scope follows causal impact rather than the farthest destination visited, not because campaigns must remain geographically local.
