# Each campaign begins with a new player-created Captain

Every campaign normally begins with Captain Creation rather than selecting a prior protagonist from the Crew Archive. The new Captain's appearance, origin, starting capabilities, and personal drive give the run its central identity and provide material for the Campaign Director; two archived Crewmates complete the Starting Crew, while completed or permanently lost Captains remain in campaign history rather than becoming Crewmate unlocks.

ADR 0042 supersedes `Captain` as a required Player Character role. The accepted boundary that every campaign begins with a newly created protagonist, supported by two archived starting Crewmates, remains unchanged.

ADR 0070 supersedes the exclusion of surviving completed Player Characters from future Crewmate eligibility. A new Player Character is still created for every campaign, while surviving prior protagonists may return only as Crewmates.
