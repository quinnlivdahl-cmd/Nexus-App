# Climax order effects are dynamically replanned

When the Climax Set locks, the Campaign Director creates a bounded Climax Plan of cross-node dependency hooks, possible advantage and pressure lanes, Playable Finale requirements, and permitted order-effect operations rather than scripting all six possible orders. After each of the first two climax nodes, the Director replans from the actual committed outcome: every completed node must grant at least one meaningful persistent advantage for later play and worsen, increase the risk or cost of, or close at least one opportunity or approach elsewhere. Updates cite their outcome source, affected dependency, remaining prospect, permitted operation, and player-visible signal; deterministic validation commits them without replacing the three locked node identities or allowing model fiat.
