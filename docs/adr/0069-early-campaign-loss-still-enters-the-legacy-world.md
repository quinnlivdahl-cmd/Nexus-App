# Early campaign loss still enters the Legacy World

Player Character Permanent Loss before Node 10 ends active campaign play and triggers Early Campaign Resolution rather than deleting or ignoring the run. The normal structured closure call records the terminal cause, World Calendar range, surviving crew statuses, accumulated Game Truth, material unresolved threads, and eligible bounded Legacy Consequences without claiming a Playable Finale occurred; a separate narration call may then present the epilogue. Validated consequences promote automatically into the selected Legacy World's Saga Source, so failure shapes future world state alongside successful completed campaigns.
