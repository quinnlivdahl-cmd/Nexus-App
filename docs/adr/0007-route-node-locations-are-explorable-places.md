# Route Node Locations are explorable places

Each Route Node opens a compact multi-room Location that supports Free Movement, Points of Interest, environmental identity, and optional discovery in addition to one or more periods of Tactical Pressure. The minimum exploration promise is three connected Areas, one distinctive sight or identity, two optional Points of Interest, one Field-Team-sensitive interaction, and meaningful Free Movement before Tactical Pressure dominates. Turn-Based Mode remains the mechanical centerpiece of a node, but most Locations must feel like places with reasons to explore rather than tactical arenas waiting for a fight.
