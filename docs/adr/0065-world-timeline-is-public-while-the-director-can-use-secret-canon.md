# World Timeline is public while the Director can use secret canon

World Timeline ticks expose only public Legacy Consequences. Saga Source entries carry explicit Canon Visibility and permitted-reveal policy, so secret, restricted, and character-specific consequences remain authoritative and retrievable for relevant Campaign Director planning without becoming public timeline lore. Context Broker packets for dialogue, narration, and other Generated Performance still enforce character knowledge and scene permissions; Director access cannot grant universal NPC knowledge or leak a secret through an unauthorized speaker. Developer Mode traces secret selection and omission while normal play remains redacted.

Canon Visibility changes only through a played and validated reveal such as a discovery, confession, leak, or committed consequence. The transition commits atomically with its revealing event, cites the prior secret and reveal source, and updates only justified knowledge scopes; Director planning or prose alone cannot publish a secret.
