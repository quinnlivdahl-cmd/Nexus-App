# Prototype campaigns become fixtures and modular source

The Rook and Nexus Primer prototype campaigns do not ship as fixed default campaigns. Their existing flows survive as Campaign Fixtures for regression-testing rules, dialogue continuity, route adaptation, state recovery, and harness behavior, while reviewed characters, situations, factions, Locations, and consequences may be extracted into validated modular content for the dynamically directed game.
