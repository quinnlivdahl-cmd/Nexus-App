# Overlapping triggers compose one Tactical Pressure period

Every simultaneously active Tactical Pressure Trigger inside one Location composes into a single continuous Tactical Pressure period. Tactical Participation and Timing Entries become the de-duplicated union of active trigger selections; existing actors keep their Initiative, current round state, and spent resources, while newly participating actors use the late-entry rule and receive the applicable Surprise Evaluation. A new hazard, objective, pursuit, or hostile development therefore expands ordered play instead of starting a second Encounter authority or rerolling the existing sequence, and the period ends only when every overlapping trigger clears.
