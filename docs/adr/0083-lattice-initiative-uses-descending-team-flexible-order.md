# Lattice Initiative uses descending team-flexible order

Nexus translates the desired Baldur's Gate 3 Initiative behavior into its own resolution backbone instead of importing a literal d4. Every participating actor makes an individual Lattice-100 Initiative Roll. The d100 maps to a four-step value: 01–25 = 4, 26–50 = 3, 51–75 = 2, and 76–100 = 1. The actor's Initiative Score is that value plus its Dexterity modifier and Initiative bonuses, and actors are ranked from highest Initiative Score to lowest. This keeps lower Lattice rolls favorable while preserving the desired compact Initiative range. An opposing tie favors the actor with higher Dexterity; if Dexterity is also equal, the tied opposing actors make visible unmodified d100 tie-break rolls where lower wins and only an exact tie rerolls. Consecutive same-alliance entries form a Shared Initiative Block whose individual activations may be interleaved by that alliance's controller. The block rule applies symmetrically to every alliance; individual AP, MP, reactions, and other resources remain actor-owned, and a block cannot cross another alliance or a hazard or objective timing entry.
