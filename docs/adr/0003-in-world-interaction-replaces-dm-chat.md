# In-world interaction replaces DM chat

DM chat is not the primary Nexus gameplay interface. A contextual Intent Bar accepts unrestricted Player Intent when deliberately summoned during Free Movement, inside character-focused Freeform Dialogue, or through the validated Improvise action during Turn-Based Mode; spatial prompts and concise Area Introductions complete the game-native interaction surface without assistant-product framing. Generated Performance must preserve distinct character voice and knowledge boundaries, avoid generic assistant behavior and redundant prose, and never invent or commit Game Truth.
