# Transit normally connects distinct Route Node Locations

A literal open-space Ship Transit normally follows each Route Choice and connects the current Route Node Location to the next one. Same-orbit travel within one body's Region is common, while transitions may also span distant bodies such as Mars and Neptune; only rare transitions between distinct playable sections of one enormous structure omit open-space travel. Transit is the journey itself, applies declared Travel Consequences through deterministic rules, and may carry pre-node Campaign Director preparation, while a Transit Event is an optional authored or generated situation rather than a required interruption on every trip.
