# Check Opportunities are team-aware and knowledge-safe

Spatial opportunity recognition considers the deployed Field Team so the player does not cycle characters merely to discover possible interactions. Recognizable objects and optional general markers expose only known actions; useful correctable restrictions may be shown with their reason, while secret actions remain absent. A deliberate Check defaults to the controlled eligible character but lets the player select another eligible lead and qualified assistance, never silently substituting the best odds. The baseline preview shows known chance, modifiers, and broad risk, while abilities or equipment may reveal additional validated information.
