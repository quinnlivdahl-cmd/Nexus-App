# A hidden Campaign Director drives the adaptive arc

The text-playtest DM evolves into a behind-the-scenes Campaign Director rather than a visible chat persona. It maintains unresolved Campaign Threads and pressures, creates at least three meaningful Route Prospects at each juncture, supplies dramatic context to dialogue and narration, and adapts later Locations to validated player outcomes. Dialogue Outcomes may alter relationships, leads, Campaign Threads, and future Route Prospects after validation; the Director may compose from approved content and improvise performance, but it cannot override mechanics, retcon Game Truth, or force a predetermined story path.
