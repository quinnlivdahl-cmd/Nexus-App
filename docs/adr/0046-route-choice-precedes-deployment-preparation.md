# Route Choice precedes Deployment Preparation

Validated Route Prospects appear at the start of Downtime. Browsing and preview selection remain reversible, but an explicit confirmation atomically and irreversibly commits the next Route Choice through normal consequence-preserving play before the player selects the Field Team, character loadouts, and Ship Assignments for that known destination. Once Deployment Preparation is finalized and the crew departs, Transit begins and the pre-node Campaign Director sequence receives the selected route and final deployment context to prepare the Node Brief. This preserves informed preparation without accidental single-click commitment, generating full unchosen Locations, or planning against a crew configuration that later changes.

For Route Node 1, ADR 0071 inserts Node 0 Advancement after Route Choice confirmation and before Deployment Preparation.
