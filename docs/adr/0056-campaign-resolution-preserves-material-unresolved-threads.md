# Campaign Resolution preserves material unresolved threads

The Resolution Brief classifies every material Campaign Thread as resolved, changed, or unresolved instead of fabricating off-screen closure to make the ending appear complete. It also gives every surviving Crew Roster member a compact ending status. The epilogue expands only threads and characters that materially shaped the campaign's choices, relationships, pressures, Playable Finale, or lasting aftermath. Unresolved threads may therefore remain honest parts of the completed campaign record without forcing obligatory prose or becoming active cross-campaign plots by default.
