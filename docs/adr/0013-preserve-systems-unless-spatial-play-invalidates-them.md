# Preserve systems unless spatial play invalidates them

The revised Nexus vision does not reopen every existing rule or content system. Mechanics, progression, lore, equipment, character structure, and other established work survive by default when animated Free Movement, persistent Locations, seamless Turn-Based Mode, or the hidden Campaign Director do not materially affect their assumptions; systems are evolved or removed only when the spatial-game transition contradicts or invalidates them.
