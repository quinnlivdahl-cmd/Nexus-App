# Validated Legacy Consequences promote automatically

At Campaign Resolution, every proposed Legacy Consequence that passes source, authority, scope, contradiction, Canon Visibility, and Setting Pillar validation promotes automatically into the Legacy World's Saga Source. Normal play has no separate approval or veto step for earned world changes. The player may inspect consequences through campaign history, the public World Timeline when applicable, and Developer Mode, but inspection, export, or readable-chronicle regeneration cannot alter canonical entries; corrupt or invalid state uses explicit recovery tooling rather than narrative preference.

ADR 0069 includes eligible consequences from Early Campaign Resolution rather than discarding failed campaigns from Legacy World history.
