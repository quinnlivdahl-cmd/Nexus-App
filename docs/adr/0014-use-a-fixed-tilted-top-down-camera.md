# Use a fixed Tilted Top-Down camera

Free Movement and Turn-Based Mode share a fixed camera that reads primarily as top-down with only a shallow tilt for volume and depth. The camera may follow, pan, zoom modestly, pull back for tactical readability, and fade obstructing architecture, but it does not freely rotate or switch to a separate tactical perspective; this protects modular asset reuse, cover readability, and achievable indie production scope.
