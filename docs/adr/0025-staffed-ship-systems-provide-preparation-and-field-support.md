# Staffed Ship Systems provide preparation and Field Team support

An undeployed Crewmate may receive a Ship Assignment to Navigation, Sensors, Engineering and Fabrication, Medical and Continuity, or Communications and Network for the next Route Node. Each staffed Ship System grants both a concrete Preparation Benefit and a limited player-invoked Ship Support Action during the node; system capability determines available support, the assigned Crewmate's relevant skills influence Checks or effectiveness, and charges, range, signal, or node conditions keep support from replacing the three-character Field Team.

ADR 0046 clarifies timing: the player commits the Route Choice before selecting the Field Team, loadout, and Ship Assignments for that known destination.
