# Player Character identity and Ship ownership are separate

Every campaign begins with a newly created Player Character who always remains the group's narrative and decision leader but is not required to be a literal Captain. Captain is an optional character role tied to Ship ownership and command: a Player Character Captain directly selects an eligible Ship Frame, while a selected Captain Crewmate brings their profile-bound Ship without a separate frame choice or replacing the Player Character as group leader. If neither starting character is a Captain, the Campaign Director uses Node 0 fiction such as theft, a sympathetic gift, or improvised transport to provide an eligible campaign Ship by resolution without making acquisition the node's normal premise. Every campaign therefore retains its Ship, Ship Systems, and between-node activities while ownership-neutral Downtime does not imply that the Player Character owns the vessel or that the group always returns to it between nodes.

ADR 0070 permits a surviving former Player Character to return later as a Crewmate, but this never transfers group leadership away from the newly created Player Character.
