# Ship damage is a persistent narrative condition

The Ship has no hull-health attrition or component-damage simulation. Damage arises from narrative events and validated outcomes as a persistent Ship Condition with a specific campaign effect—for example, damaged engines blocking longer-distance Route Prospects—and remains until a Ship Condition Resolution uses an appropriate Crewmate, Lattice Check, and stated cost or opportunity to clear it.
