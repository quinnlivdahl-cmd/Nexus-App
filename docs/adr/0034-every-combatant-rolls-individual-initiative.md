# Every combatant rolls individual Initiative

The provisional alternating-activation baseline is replaced by true individual Initiative: when Tactical Pressure begins, every relevant combatant makes one Lattice-100 Initiative Roll and enters a fixed ordered turn sequence until that pressure ends. A later period of Tactical Pressure rolls a fresh order; Initiative remains a buildable character capability so traits, abilities, equipment, awareness, circumstances, and crew composition can support playstyles such as an exceptionally fast-acting Field Team.
