# A normal run builds a seven-character Crew Roster

A campaign begins with the Captain and two starting Crewmates, then normally adds four Crewmates across the first nine Route Nodes and reaches the finale with seven total characters. Recruitment Offers may require choosing among candidates, while other nodes reward different forms of progression; the Campaign Director may add a replacement opportunity after a death or departure rather than enforcing a rigid node-by-node recruitment schedule.
