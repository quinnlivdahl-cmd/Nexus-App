# A normal run builds a seven-character Crew Roster

A campaign begins with the Player Character and two Starting Crewmates, then normally adds four Crewmates across the first nine Route Nodes and reaches the finale with seven total characters. Recruitment Offers may require choosing among candidates, while other nodes reward different forms of progression; the Campaign Director may add a replacement opportunity after a death or departure rather than enforcing a rigid node-by-node recruitment schedule. A Crewmate who joins after campaign start enters at the campaign's current level with a predetermined legal level-appropriate Campaign Build rather than requiring the player to recreate every missed advancement choice. Future advancement choices become player-controlled after recruitment.
