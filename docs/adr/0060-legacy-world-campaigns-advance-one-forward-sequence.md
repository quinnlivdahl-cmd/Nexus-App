# Legacy World campaigns advance one forward sequence

Campaigns within a Legacy World form one forward Legacy Sequence. Each new campaign begins after all previously completed campaigns in that world and inherits their current non-superseded Legacy Consequences. The initial contract does not support parallel campaigns, prequels, inserting a campaign before completed history, or branching timelines. Forward order does not itself require literal calendar dates or exact travel durations; that chronology representation remains a separate decision.

ADR 0061 resolves that separate decision by adding a literal World Calendar for lore while retaining abstract gameplay clocks and consequence-focused route presentation.
