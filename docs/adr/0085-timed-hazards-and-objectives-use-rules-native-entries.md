# Timed hazards and objectives use rules-native entries

An active Location hazard or objective that changes with ordered time receives a deterministic Timing Entry in the same sequence as participating actors, resolving at the end of the round by default or at another authored rank or cadence when its rules require one. The entry references the existing Location entity, makes no Initiative Roll, and receives no AP, MP, or other actor action resources. Its effects resolve through the existing validation, Effect, StateDelta, transaction, and commit rules so environmental timing cannot become a bespoke Encounter script or a second mutation authority.
