# Crew Rosters reset while the Crew Archive persists

The active Crew Roster belongs to one campaign run and resets when a new run begins, while the Crew Archive persistently records discovered and recruited Crewmates. A new Captain chooses two eligible Crewmates from that historical pool; returning Crewmates are the same people and may record no more than three validated Archive Memories per completed campaign, but their level, equipment, temporary upgrades, and ability development reset into a level-1 Campaign Build. The Archive preserves player-facing history, while a Crewmate's subjective memory after Permanent Loss depends on the in-fiction recovery method.
