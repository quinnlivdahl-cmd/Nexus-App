# In-Location recruitment preserves a three-character Field Team

An accepted in-Location recruit may replace either non-Player-Character Field Team member without expanding the directly controlled party beyond three characters. The displaced Crewmate holds a grounded Location Support Assignment at their actual committed position when the swap occurs, and the player may reverse or change the swap only by physically returning there during Free Movement; the swap itself never relocates an actor, and remote roster swaps or default composition changes during Tactical Pressure are not permitted.

At departure, every actor the player intends to extract must be present at the extraction point or have a validated safe route for Rally to Extraction. Rally may use automatic pathing but never teleport an actor. The player may instead deliberately leave a living Crewmate or accepted recruit behind whether or not a safe Rally route existed, but departure must commit the grounded consequence.

A living Crewmate deliberately left behind becomes Stranded rather than automatically suffering Permanent Loss. The campaign preserves the character's identity, exact Actor State, last known Location, and known circumstances while removing them from the active Crew Roster; death, capture, escape, rescue, or another later outcome requires its own committed evidence.
