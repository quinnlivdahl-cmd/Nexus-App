# Late entrants join Initiative without interrupting

An actor that joins Tactical Participation after the ordered sequence begins makes its individual Initiative Roll and enters the existing order at its normal ranked slot. Joining never interrupts or replays the current activation: the actor may act in the current round only when its ranked slot has not yet passed, otherwise its first activation waits until the next round. Existing participants keep their relative order, and joining alone grants no immediate action outside normal reaction or explicit interrupt rules.
