# Individual variation uses Aptitude and Growth Traits

The preserved IV/EV-inspired character direction becomes two explicit trait layers rather than hidden numerical breeding-style values. Aptitude Traits are persistent individual strengths, potential, or weaknesses that remain with archived Crewmates and are selected for a new Captain; Recruitment Offers must provide a fair way to reveal all mechanically relevant Aptitudes before an irreversible choice. Growth Traits are usage-informed feat-like choices within the run-specific Campaign Build and reset between adventures; their exact advancement timing and cost remain deferred until Nexus defines that progression surface, and they do not replace Attributes, Skills, Skill Focuses, Abilities, or ordinary Traits and Techniques.
