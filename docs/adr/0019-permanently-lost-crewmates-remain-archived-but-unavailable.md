# Permanently lost Crewmates remain archived but unavailable

Permanent Loss removes a Crewmate from the current Crew Roster and makes them unavailable as a future starting choice, but their Crew Archive history is not deleted. Rare later campaign content may recover or reconstruct the same person; by default they return from the Continuity Checkpoint established at the start of the fatal campaign and do not subjectively remember that run, although an exceptional recovery method may explicitly preserve more.
