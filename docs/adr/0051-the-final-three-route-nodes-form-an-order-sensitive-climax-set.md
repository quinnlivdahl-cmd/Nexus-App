# The final three Route Nodes form an order-sensitive Climax Set

After Route Node 7 resolves and before Node 8 is selected, the Campaign Director proposes a Climax Set of three distinct prospect identities that the player will complete once each as Nodes 8, 9, and 10 in any order. All three remain available as an exception to normal prospect lapse, but earlier climax outcomes must materially change the remaining nodes' risks, costs, benefits, or approaches through committed state and declared dependencies. Each member is designed to host the Playable Finale without preassembling its full Location, and the last remaining member becomes the Finale Node where accumulated Game Truth and order consequences shape the campaign's climactic peak.

ADR 0052 makes `Climax Set` and `Finale Node` internal terms. The player should understand the three-front endgame and order stakes from campaign convergence and fiction rather than explicit meta labels or a tutorial explanation.

ADR 0053 requires the Director to replan order effects from committed climax outcomes rather than executing a fixed permutation script.

ADR 0054 separates the Playable Finale inside Node 10 from the Campaign Resolution and epilogue that follow it.
