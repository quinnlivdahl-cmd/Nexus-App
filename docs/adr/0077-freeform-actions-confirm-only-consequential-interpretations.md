# Freeform Actions confirm only consequential interpretations

During Free Movement, a safely interpreted Freeform Action executes without an extra confirmation when it is routine, reversible, and carries no meaningful Check, scarce cost, known material risk, or irreversible consequence. When any of those stakes are present, Nexus presents a concise player-safe preview of the interpreted action and requires confirmation before resolution. The preview identifies the acting character, target, approach, costs, applicable Check and Target Score, and known immediate stakes so the player can verify both interpretation and mechanics. Turn-Based Improvise always previews its interpretation and tactical cost before execution. Hidden modifiers, undiscovered consequences, and secret information remain hidden; confirmation exposes only what the acting character could reasonably know.
