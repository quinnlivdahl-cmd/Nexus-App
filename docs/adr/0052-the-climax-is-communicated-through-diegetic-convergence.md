# The climax is communicated through diegetic convergence

`Climax Set` and `Finale Node` are internal contract and Developer Mode terms rather than normal-player labels. The three final fronts must converge from the established Main Campaign Thread, factions, Route Pressure, and committed outcomes so that, by the transition into Node 8, the player can reasonably understand why all three require resolution and why delaying one will make it the site of the final playable culmination. Every member must reference at least one earlier player-observable seed; one may become clearly actionable only after Node 7, but none may originate solely from unexposed Director State. The game communicates this through campaign progress, character dialogue, incentives, pressure, and framing instead of announcing `the final three` or explaining the finale-selection rule in tutorial language.
