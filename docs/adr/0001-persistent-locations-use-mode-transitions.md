# Persistent locations use seamless mode transitions

Nexus does not divide spatial play into discrete Encounter objects or encounter maps. A persistent Location remains authoritative while Tactical Pressure shifts play from Free Movement into Turn-Based Mode and back again, preserving positions and world state; the transition occurs whenever position, timing, and action order become consequential, not only for combat. Turn-Based Mode governs time across the whole Location while granting turns only to relevant actors, hazards, and objectives. Local Aftermath resolves in place, and campaign outcomes are consolidated only during Route Node Resolution when the crew departs. Authored gameplay is therefore composed from triggers, participants, objectives, behaviors, and resolution conditions rather than isolated encounter containers.
