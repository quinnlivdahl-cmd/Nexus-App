# Model spend caps protect active-scene completion

A player-configured model-spend cap includes a protected Scene Completion Reserve rather than stopping generative play in the middle of an active scene. Nexus admits a model-dependent scene only when its calibrated reserve is available, conservatively prices each call before dispatch, disables optional generation first, and spends the reserve only on calls needed to resolve the active scene and create a durable consequence-preserving checkpoint. The cap is never exceeded; after the checkpoint, Nexus blocks another model-dependent scene and the next Route Node while retaining deterministic save, review, export, and configuration access.

Before the Playable Finale, the protected reserve also includes the required Campaign Resolution closure and epilogue calls so a completed campaign is not stranded without its validated ending record.

The reserve likewise protects Early Campaign Resolution after Player Character Permanent Loss.
