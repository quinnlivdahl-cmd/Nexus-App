# Unchosen Route Prospects normally lapse

When the player confirms a Route Choice, the unchosen Route Prospects normally lapse and leave the active choice pool rather than forming a persistent quest list. Reoffering the same underlying opportunity is exceptional and must create a new prospect linked to the prior offer, cite a committed reason for returning, and expose a material reduction in benefit or increase in risk or cost. Lapsed history remains available for targeted recall and Developer Mode, but the Campaign Director cannot recycle an identical option or silently restore it unchanged.

ADR 0051 creates the explicit exception: all three members of the Climax Set remain available until played, but committed outcomes must materially update the risks, costs, benefits, or approaches of the remaining members.
