# Aged-out Crewmates remain living world characters

When Biological Age or another explicit continuity rule makes a Crewmate unavailable for field play, the character becomes a Retired Crewmate rather than being deleted or treated as dead. They remain a living Legacy World person and Crew Archive entry who may appear as a contact, mentor, relationship figure, faction participant, or other NPC. Retirement, archive status, campaign absence, and context omission never imply death. The Campaign Director cannot kill them off-screen; the current simple natural-death baseline commits death at Biological Age 100, while other death requires played and validated events. Detailed retirement mechanics are deferred beyond the vertical slice.
