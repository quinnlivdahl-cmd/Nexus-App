# Ship Systems change through scarce modules

Ship Systems do not use a detailed player-managed repair tree or incremental stat-point progression. Scarce Ship Modules unlock qualitative capabilities and support actions, may compete for limited system slots, and enter play through Route Node salvage and rewards, faction or Campaign Thread outcomes, and Transit Events such as ship stores; routine repairs remain automatic unless a specific consequence makes them meaningful.
