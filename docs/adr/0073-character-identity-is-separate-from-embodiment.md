# Character identity is separate from Embodiment

A Character Profile represents the continuing person independently of their current Embodiment. Embodiment owns the current body or platform and its physical state, including Bioform when applicable, Chassis tier, Installed Cyberware, durable gene modifications, body compatibility, and related consequences; replacing a sleeve, shell, drone body, or Full Chassis body does not create a new person or archive identity. During Player Character Creation the player selects an eligible Starting Embodiment when the concept permits it, while each Starting Crewmate receives a predetermined profile-bound Starting Embodiment and a converted former Player Character retains the current committed Embodiment they had at campaign end. `Chassis` remains reserved for cybernetic integration tier rather than becoming a synonym for body.
