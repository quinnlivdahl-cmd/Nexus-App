# Crewmates age but Stasis suspends biological aging

Crewmates have World Calendar age and deterministic Biological Age governed by non-stasis elapsed time. Long Ship Transits normally use Stasis, advancing lore chronology without equivalent biological aging; validated Transit Events may interrupt stasis without aging the entire journey. Campaigns and between-campaign gaps are normally short enough that age-based unavailability remains uncommon even across many campaigns, and real-world product lifetime never advances the Legacy World. The campaign-start validator protects already selected Starting Crew, while the current simple natural-death baseline commits death at Biological Age 100 rather than through Campaign Director judgment.

ADR 0067 defines aging out as retirement from field play rather than deletion or death.

Detailed aging and retirement mechanics are deferred beyond the vertical slice. The Campaign Director cannot author off-screen Crewmate death.
