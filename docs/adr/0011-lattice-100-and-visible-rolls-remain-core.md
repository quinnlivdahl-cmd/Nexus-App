# Lattice-100 and visible rolls remain core

Lattice-100 survives as Nexus's authoritative d100 resolution backbone, and the roll remains a visible part of play rather than disappearing behind percentage previews. Nexus targets a high frequency of meaningful Check Opportunities across exploration, Freeform Dialogue, and Turn-Based Mode; the Baldur's Gate 3 comparison concerns that frequency, not its modal dice-screen execution. Lattice Rolls appear through a compact non-modal corner ticker while play continues, routine actions may resolve without a roll, and Generated Performance may describe only validated results. Failed hidden Checks appear by default as unidentified missed opportunities that reveal no secret content, with a player setting available to conceal them. Passive Checks roll independently for eligible Field Team members, while a Deliberate Check uses one selected lead with qualified assistance rather than repeated attempts by the whole party.
