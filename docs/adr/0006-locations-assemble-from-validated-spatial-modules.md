# Locations assemble from validated spatial modules

The TacMap collage approach evolves into an engine-native Location assembly system. Designers author reusable Location Modules and compatible Location Blueprints with validated geometry, navigation, interaction sockets, occlusion, and Cover Positions; the Campaign Director may choose a blueprint and apply approved faction, hazard, prop, lighting, damage, and Generated Performance layers, but generated imagery or prose cannot define authoritative spatial geometry.
