# Enemy tactical intent is model-proposed and locally executed

When Tactical Pressure begins, the model may propose one bounded Tactical Directive describing an enemy group's goal, posture, priorities, restraints, and authored contingencies through approved machine-readable values. Deterministic enemy logic executes legal movement, targeting, actions, reactions, and contingency transitions throughout the situation; an optional freeform rationale is explanatory only, and the vertical slice does not call a model for each enemy turn or permit generated tactics to bypass rules and Game Truth.
