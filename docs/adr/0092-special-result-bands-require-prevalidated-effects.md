# Special result bands require prevalidated effects

The five-band noncombat Lattice ladder remains the default result-band set, but a Check does not invent special outcomes merely to fill every band. Before rolling, Rules Core validates each supported outcome: Strong Success requires a meaningful added benefit, Partial Success requires one grounded adverse After Effect, and Hard Failure requires a serious grounded adverse After Effect. An unsupported Strong Success or Partial Success collapses to ordinary Success, while an unsupported Hard Failure collapses to ordinary Failure. By default, a valid Partial applies the prevalidated After Effect most directly caused by the player's approach; only an explicit ability or equipment rule lets the player reduce, replace, redirect, or choose it. Combat Graze remains governed by the separate attack result-band set.
