# Timeline and Historical Eras

## Date policy

Do not invent exact dates in this pass. Use eras and causal sequence until a dedicated timeline reconciliation is completed.

The working era is roughly several centuries beyond the present, with previous drafts using a "roughly 300 years in the future" frame. Keep that frame provisional.

## Provisional causal sequence

1. **Acceleration century**: AI, drones, climate pressure, energy stress, biotech, cybernetics, and logistics systems intensify governance strain.
2. **Drone Wars / WW3**: long multi-theater conflict worsens climate, shatters old assumptions about state monopoly on force, and accelerates corporate-government sovereignty.
3. **End of state monopoly on war**: states survive, but drones, counter-drones, orbital infrastructure, cyberwarfare, mercenaries, proxies, corporate logistics, and AI targeting permanently change force projection.
4. **War medicine and cybernetic normalization**: prosthetics, operator interfaces, drone control, industrial augmentation, neural interfaces, and battlefield medicine evolve into broader cybernetic systems.
5. **Commercial brain augmentation crisis**: therapeutic implants become productivity, cognition, memory, attention, and labor-market tools, creating pressure to upgrade to remain employable or protected.
6. **Personhood Wars**: disputes over uploads, splicers, AI, implants, legal identity, copied minds, and mind-editing escalate into violence and legal fracture.
7. **C-POH terror spark / legitimacy transition**: a human-continuity movement emerges from terror, backlash, and real social fear. Its path from violent spark to Earth-rooted authority remains unresolved.
8. **Treaty Freeze**: no side fully wins. System law protects some organic-human norms while corporations, upload polities, habitats, and C-POH all remain significant.
9. **Upload confirmation and violence**: secret uploads, coerced copying, legal traps, and kidnapped or duplicated persons become public enough to destabilize law.
10. **Secret Signal research backfire**: research at a major implant/cybernetics corporation, likely near the Jupiter moons, causes or reveals a disturbance through required employee brain implants.
11. **Campaign present**: the solar system sits between deniable proxy war and open war. War is near, but not tomorrow.


## Biological and personhood sequence seeds

The exact dates remain unresolved, but the causal order should preserve these pressures:

- Gene editing becomes elite, institutional, labor, medical, and habitat-adaptation technology, not one single movement.
- Designer Humans emerge from family-line prestige, curated inheritance, aristocratic/elite status, and signature traits.
- Vatborn emerge from artificial birth, designed-purpose labor, contract, caste, institutional utility, ownership history, or quasi-property systems.
- Low-G / Adapted Humans emerge from habitat and environment pressure, especially where standard Earth gravity assumptions become impractical or culturally distant.
- Ape splicers precede later splicer lines.
- Lizard splicers connect to long-duration spaceflight pressure.
- Mycelial and cephalopod peoples are newer/less settled and should remain less historically fixed until later work.

These sequence seeds should not force exact dates until the dedicated timeline reconciliation pass.

## Legacy World chronology

Within a Legacy World, campaigns and between-campaign developments occupy one literal, forward-moving calendar sequence. A new campaign start date is set after Player Character and crew selection so biological age, availability, relationships, and world developments can be evaluated against the actual selected cast. The exact real-world-era anchor may remain provisional while in-world chronology is still literal and ordered. This reconciles [ADR-0060](../../../../adr/0060-legacy-world-campaigns-advance-one-forward-sequence.md), [ADR-0061](../../../../adr/0061-legacy-worlds-use-literal-calendar-chronology.md), and [ADR-0062](../../../../adr/0062-campaign-start-dates-follow-player-and-crew-selection.md).

Between campaigns, the Director may commit bounded developments derived from elapsed time, prior consequences, established actors, and unresolved pressures. These developments advance the world; they cannot erase or contradict committed Saga Sources. This reconciles [ADR-0063](../../../../adr/0063-legacy-worlds-have-bounded-between-campaign-developments.md).

The **World Timeline** is a public reference surface, not a mandatory recap that interrupts the opening of every campaign. It records player-knowable chronology and consequences. The Director may also use secret canon in its separate hidden state, revealing it only through play. This reconciles [ADR-0064](../../../../adr/0064-world-timeline-is-reference-not-a-forced-recap.md) and [ADR-0065](../../../../adr/0065-world-timeline-is-public-while-the-director-can-use-secret-canon.md).
