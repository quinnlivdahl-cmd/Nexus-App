# Alien Signal and Contact Mystery

## Core rule

The Signal is mystery pressure, not solved alien lore.

At campaign start, first contact has not happened in a clean, public, undeniable way. The solar system has signs, anomalies, interference, coincidences, UAP-like events, implant disturbances, and data fragments. Those are enough to shape politics and paranoia, but not enough to answer the central question.

## Preferred current model

The strongest current working model is Human Threshold + Feedback Event.

The Signal may not have changed. Instead, enough implanted minds, networked cognition, brain augmentation, cybernetic infrastructure, and research activity may have made humanity capable of reflecting, amplifying, receiving, or being noticed by it.

## Choir relationship

The Choir may be connected to the Signal, but the connection is not solved.

Possible interpretations to preserve:

- The Choir is an emergent cybernetic hallucination intensified by the Signal.
- The Choir is a distributed intelligence pattern in implant networks.
- The Choir is a human institution misreading an alien phenomenon.
- The Choir is a corporate disaster caused by Secret Research Backfire.
- The Choir is what happened when implant networks became large enough to hear the Signal hearing back.

Do not define one as final without user approval.

## UAP / pre-Signal anomaly thread

UAP or UFO-like incidents may exist as continuity material, but they should preserve mystery. They can foreshadow, mislead, create archival evidence, or create political mythology without proving a clean alien visit.


## Signal pacing and crew-state interaction

Signal effects should not be pervasive at the start of the game. They should usually be later-game, discovery-gated, node-specific, encounter-specific, rare, or introduced after players have learned something about the Choir.

Working preservation notes:

- Signal Echo may be an early visible node/encounter effect, initially readable as comms weirdness, sensor noise, morale pressure, or environmental anomaly.
- Signal interaction may eventually use a Resolve-like surface, but the subsystem is parked.
- Morale or Loyalty effects may later create penalties, vulnerabilities, or complications for Signal/Contact checks.
- Signal effects should scale as worsening pressure rather than instant explanation.

This is mystery-pacing guidance, not a final Signal subsystem.

## Contact as game surface

The restricted or special Contact channel from `Skills` should remain tied to Signal interaction, anomaly perception, alien-system interface, or contact-adjacent comprehension. It is not a normal social skill.

## What to avoid

- Do not reveal the Signal's true nature casually.
- Do not make the Choir a neat alien faction unless later approved.
- Do not make every anomaly alien.
- Do not make contact so clear that the core social conflict collapses.
