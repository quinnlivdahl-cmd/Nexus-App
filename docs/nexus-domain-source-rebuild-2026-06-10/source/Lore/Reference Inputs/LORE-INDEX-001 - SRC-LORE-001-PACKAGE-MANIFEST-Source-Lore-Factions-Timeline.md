## Conflict Decisions

- Campaign-derived lore is source-candidate evidence, not automatically global canon.
- `C-POH` is preserved as a working handle, not locked final naming.
- `Choir` is the preferred working concept. `Collective` remains placeholder/outside shorthand unless promoted.
- Timeline uses eras and causal sequence unless dates are explicitly approved.
- Reference compendium material can scaffold future work but should not be promoted wholesale.

## Evidence Preserved

Lore transition evidence includes:

- broad lore/faction/timeline source;
- source-adjacent Core, Characters, Equipment, and Skills docs;
- seed handoffs and harvest capsules;
- DM playtest handoffs;
- Draft escape/counter/aftermath handoff material;
- reference lore guidance and hard-sci-fi/space-opera seed material;
- mass-intake evidence about chassis, body shaping, Standard Fit, C-POH, Splicers, Vatborn, Designer Humans, personhood, and station culture.

## Cross-Domain Routing

- `Characters` keeps character-facing origin/chassis mechanics and playable-body implications.
- `Equipment` keeps cyberware, EMP, integrated tools, Firewall/System Status, and equipment implementation.
- `Lore` keeps history, personhood, caste, law, faction ideology, station culture, Signal/Contact, and naming questions.
- `Dashboards` keep active campaign state until promoted.

## Preservation Rule

No old lore doc, faction doc, timeline doc, handoff, dashboard, seed branch, reference report, or backup is deletion-approved by this manifest.
